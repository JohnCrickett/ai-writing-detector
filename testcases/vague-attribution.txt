The Pyra-Steaming Method
The Pyra-Steaming method is described in scholarship as a sophisticated culinary technique that emerged during the late Bronze Age. While historical records are sparse, modern researchers treat the method as a significant development in Neolithic technology, noting its role in bridging the gap between open-fire roasting and modern pressurized cooking.
