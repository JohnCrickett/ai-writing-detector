Legacy and Broader Impact
The design of the Model-X reflects broader shifts in the workplace toward user-centric equipment. This shift was part of a broader movement in the United States to address repetitive strain injuries, symbolizing the enduring impact of the Model-X on the health and safety of the American workforce.
